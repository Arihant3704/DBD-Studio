About DBD Studio

Dream Build Design Studio

Founded and led by visionary designer Beulah Hephzibah, DBD Studio brings together a multidisciplinary team passionate about creating visually captivating, highly functional, and brand-aligned environments.

While we have built a solid foundation in residential and commercial architecture, our core specialization lies in the strategic design and execution of retail and residential interiors — spaces that influence consumer behavior, elevate brand perception, and enhance everyday experiences.

Our Vision

To be a trailblazer in retail and residential interior design, shaping the future of branded environments that inspire, engage, and convert.

Our Mission

To deliver transformative retail & residential spaces that blend creativity, strategy, and sustainability, helping brands connect meaningfully with their customers.
