Visionary Lead

The Vision of
Beulah Hephzibah

With a background in structural architecture and a passion for human-centric design, Beulah Hephzibah founded DBD Studio to bridge the gap between building and living.

Her philosophy is simple: A space is not just a container; it is a catalyst for performance, rest, and connection. Whether it's a high-stakes retail environment or a sanctuary for the home, every line drawn is an invitation to experience life more deeply.
